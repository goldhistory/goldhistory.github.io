Gabriels Gully

Gabriel's Gully was the start of the major gold rush. The first gold found there was found by Mr. Gabriel Reid. This is why it is still called Gabreil's Gully today.

Gabriel's Gully was about 60 miles from Dunedin. Because of this, most of the population of Dunedin went there to look for their fortunes.

The Old Prospecting Tools

There were two main tools that people took to the gold fields was a gold pan and a shovel, even thogh there was lots of other key tools there was also lots of other tools including a tent, food and many other things. One other tool that the experinced miners used was called a gold cradle or rocker box. it was used to process dirt faster.
